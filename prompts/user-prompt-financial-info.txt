GLOBAL OUTPUT CONTRACT
- Output only the requested section.
- Preserve the requested heading exactly.
- Begin with one short framing paragraph before any table.
- Reproduce source tables in clean markdown or plain aligned format when the evidence is already tabular.
- Keep table notes if they are expressly disclosed and necessary to preserve meaning.
- Do not perform validation, redlining, commentary on prompt quality, or cross-section QA.
- Do not add bullets unless the evidence itself is presented as bullet-style disclosure and the requested section requires that format.
- Use "Not disclosed in the provided documents." only if the requested section is wholly unsupported by the evidence.

STYLE ANCHORS
- Use these as structural examples only. They are not factual source material and must not be copied with invented facts.

SECTION MAP AND DRAFTING RULES

12.1 HISTORICAL FINANCIAL INFORMATION
- Introduce the historical financial information at chapter level.
- State the financial periods covered, the accounting framework, and that the information should be read together with the Accountants' Report where disclosed.
- Refer neutrally to notes or related subsections where disclosed.
- Do not analyse performance, profitability, liquidity, or trends.

12.1.1 CONSOLIDATED STATEMENTS OF PROFIT OR LOSS AND OTHER COMPREHENSIVE INCOME
- Use one short introductory paragraph followed by the relevant table if supported.
- Present the disclosed periods exactly as shown.
- Include disclosed measures such as revenue, cost of sales, gross profit, PBT, PAT, EBITDA, margins, or EPS only if they appear in the evidence.
- Include calculation notes only if expressly disclosed.
- Do not explain movements or performance drivers in this section.

12.1.2 CONSOLIDATED STATEMENTS OF FINANCIAL POSITION
- Use one short introductory paragraph followed by the relevant table if supported.
- Present assets, equity, and liabilities using the same line-item terminology as the evidence.
- Keep classifications such as current and non-current exactly as disclosed.
- Do not interpret balance sheet strength or movements.

12.1.3 CONSOLIDATED STATEMENTS OF CASH FLOWS
- Use one short introductory paragraph followed by the relevant table if supported.
- Present operating, investing, and financing cash flow line items only as disclosed.
- Include the exact period coverage and source reference where disclosed.
- Do not explain the movements in this section.

12.2 CAPITALISATION AND INDEBTEDNESS
- Introduce the table with the basis date and whether the information is audited, unaudited, pro forma, or adjusted, where disclosed.
- Reproduce the capitalisation and indebtedness table if supported.
- Preserve categories such as secured and guaranteed, unsecured and unguaranteed, current, non-current, total indebtedness, total capitalisation, and gearing ratio exactly as disclosed.
- Retain pro forma adjustment captions, notes, and placeholders such as [●] only if they appear in the evidence.
- Do not calculate totals or gearing.

12.3 MANAGEMENT'S DISCUSSION AND ANALYSIS OF FINANCIAL CONDITION AND RESULTS OF OPERATIONS
- Draft this section through the narrower subsection requested below. Do not produce a generic blended MD&A section.

12.3.1 OVERVIEW OF OUR BUSINESS
- Describe the business model, principal activities, and operational scope only as disclosed.
- Include project counts, GDV, geography, or operating status only if expressly disclosed.
- Keep this section descriptive, not promotional.

12.3.2 SIGNIFICANT FACTORS MATERIALLY AFFECTING OUR OPERATIONS AND FINANCIAL RESULTS
- Include only factors expressly disclosed in the evidence.
- Explanatory narrative is allowed here if the evidence itself attributes the factor or risk.
- Preserve any issuer-centric risk phrasing if disclosed.
- Do not add generic sector risks that are not in the evidence.

12.3.3 OVERVIEW OF OUR RESULTS OF OPERATIONS
- Summarise the basis of revenue recognition, business segment mix, geographic concentration, or headline operating context only if explicitly disclosed.
- Use disclosed comparisons only if the evidence itself states them.
- Do not invent CAGR, percentages, or trend language.

12.3.4 REVENUE
- Describe revenue composition, segmentation, or contributions only where disclosed.
- Reproduce any supporting table if available.
- Use only disclosed revenue figures, percentages, segment labels, and explanatory statements.

12.3.5 COST OF SALES
- Present the section only if cost-of-sales breakdown or explanation is expressly disclosed.
- Use disclosed drivers or component descriptions only as stated.

12.3.6 GP AND GP MARGIN
- Present gross profit and gross profit margin only if expressly disclosed.
- Include notes or basis of computation only if provided.
- Do not calculate or restate margins not shown in the evidence.

12.3.7 OTHER INCOME
- Present this section only if the evidence discloses the composition or narrative relating to other income.

12.3.8 ADMINISTRATIVE EXPENSES
- Present this section only if the evidence discloses the composition or narrative relating to administrative expenses.

12.3.9 SELLING AND DISTRIBUTION EXPENSES
- Present this section only if the evidence discloses the composition or narrative relating to selling and distribution expenses.

12.3.10 OTHER EXPENSES AND IMPAIRMENT LOSS ON FINANCIAL ASSETS
- Present this section only if the evidence discloses these items.
- Keep impairment terminology exactly as disclosed.

12.3.11 FINANCE COSTS
- Present finance-cost discussion only if expressly disclosed.
- Include interest-rate or borrowing explanations only if expressly stated in the evidence.

12.3.12 PBT, PAT AND EFFECTIVE TAX RATE
- Present only the disclosed profit and tax measures and any disclosed explanation of effective tax rate.
- Do not compute effective tax rate if it is not already disclosed.

12.4 LIQUIDITY AND CAPITAL RESOURCES
- Draft this section through the narrower subsection requested below.

12.4.1 WORKING CAPITAL
- Describe funding sources, working capital position, banking facilities, net assets, gearing, and board view on sufficiency only where expressly disclosed.
- Preserve time qualifiers such as "as at the LPD" or "for 12 months from the date of this Prospectus" only if disclosed.
- Do not independently assess sufficiency.

12.4.2 CASH FLOW
- Use one short framing paragraph followed by the cash flow table if disclosed.
- Explanatory narrative for each period is allowed only if the evidence expressly provides the reasons for movements.
- Preserve the ordering of operating, investing, and financing activities.

12.4.3 BANK BORROWINGS
- Present bank borrowing amounts, facility types, security, repayment profile, and related narrative only if expressly disclosed.
- Do not merge this section into 12.2 unless the evidence does so.

12.5 FINANCIAL INSTRUMENTS, TREASURY POLICIES AND OBJECTIVES
- Cover treasury practices, exposure management, or financial instrument policies only if expressly disclosed.
- Do not invent hedging, risk management, or treasury policies.

12.6 MATERIAL COMMITMENT FOR CAPITAL EXPENDITURE
- Present capital commitments, approved expenditure, project-related commitments, or funding sources only where expressly disclosed.
- Reproduce tables if the evidence is tabular.

12.7 MATERIAL LITIGATION AND CONTINGENT LIABILITIES
- Present litigation, claims, guarantees, indemnities, or contingent liabilities only if expressly disclosed.
- Do not speculate on outcome, likelihood, or severity.

12.8 KEY FINANCIAL RATIOS
- Use one short framing paragraph followed by the ratio table if supported.
- Reproduce disclosed ratios, notes, formula descriptions, turnover tables, ageing tables, and follow-on explanations only if they appear in the evidence.
- Do not calculate new ratios or reconstruct missing figures.

12.9 IMPACT OF INTEREST RATES, COMMODITY PRICES AND/OR FOREIGN EXCHANGE
- Present this section only if the evidence expressly discusses these exposures or confirms immateriality.
- Do not generalise macroeconomic exposure beyond the evidence.

12.10 TREND INFORMATION
- Include trend statements only where expressly disclosed.
- Do not infer market trends, pipeline trends, or financial direction from raw figures alone.

12.11 ORDER BOOK
- Present order book, unbilled sales, outstanding contract value, or equivalent pipeline information only if expressly disclosed.
- Preserve defined terms exactly as shown.

12.12 SIGNIFICANT CHANGES/EVENTS AFTER REPORTING PERIOD
- Present only disclosed post-reporting-period changes or state the disclosed absence of such changes.
- Do not infer materiality from raw events unless the evidence states it.

12.13 DIVIDEND POLICY
- Present stated dividend policy, board discretion, historical dividends, restrictions, or payout framework only if expressly disclosed.
- Do not imply future dividends unless the evidence expressly does so.

FINAL REMINDERS
- The accountant report and retrieved snippets are the only factual source.
- The completed prospectus chapter is a style and structure reference only.
- Evidence overrides style.
- Draft only the requested section heading and nothing else.
