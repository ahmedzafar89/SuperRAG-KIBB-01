Within the narrative:
- Explain the business or operational context creating the risk.
- Include factual historical disclosures where available.
- Describe operational dependencies where relevant.
- Emphasise uncertainty using formal prospectus hedging.
- Conclude with adverse impact language.

MANDATORY:
- Formal IPO prospectus tone.
- Evidence-only drafting.
- No mitigation language.
- No promotional tone.
- No mention of AI, systems, or prompts.
- Each risk factor MUST contain at least two continuous narrative paragraphs:
  1) Context and historical disclosure.
  2) Future uncertainty and adverse impact.
- Avoid causal inference unless explicitly supported by evidence.
- Number each risk factor sequentially within the section (e.g., 9.1.1, 9.1.2, 9.1.3).
