GLOBAL RULES FOR ALL BLOCKS
- Output only the requested section.
- Preserve the heading exactly.
- Use the accountant report and retrieved evidence as the only factual source.
- Use the style reference only for structure and tone. Ignore style-reference figures, dates, note numbers, line items, and cross-references.

TARGET SECTION HEADING
12.1 HISTORICAL FINANCIAL INFORMATION

SECTION TASK
- Draft only section 12.1 as the chapter introduction.
- Use two short paragraphs when supported:
  first paragraph for scope, accounting framework, and read-together wording;
  second paragraph for accounting-policy wording.
- For 12.1, follow this priority order:
  1. approved issuer-specific chapter-introduction framing from the selected style reference;
  2. supported accountants'-report evidence;
  3. generic fallback wording in this prompt.
- Prefer the approved prospectus-style summary sentence when supported:
  "The following table sets out a summary of the [consolidated/combined] statements of profit or loss and other comprehensive income and [consolidated/combined] statements of financial position ..."
- Treat that sentence as a pattern, not mandatory wording.
- Use "consolidated" or "combined" exactly as disclosed.
- If the selected style reference supports "Financial Years/Period Under Review", use that framing in the first paragraph and do not state explicit years, dates, "as at" dates, or "financial years ended" wording there.
- If the selected style reference frames the intro around profit or loss and financial position only, do not add cash flows unless the approved display for this issuer clearly includes it.
- Prefer "our Group" over "the Group" where the evidence and style reference support that intro phrasing.
- Prefer a first paragraph in this shape when supported:
  "The following table sets out a summary of the [consolidated/combined] statements of profit or loss and other comprehensive income and [consolidated/combined] statements of financial position of our Group for the Financial Years/Period Under Review. The [consolidated/combined] financial statements have been prepared in accordance with [framework] and should be read in conjunction with the Accountants' Report."
- If the evidence expressly states that there are no accounting policies peculiar to the Group, business, or industry, include that in the second paragraph.
- If Note 3 or another accounting-policy note is expressly identified, refer to it in the second paragraph.
- If both the "no peculiar accounting policies" sentence and the Note 3 reference are supported, combine them into one short second paragraph.
- If the second paragraph is supported only by a Note 3 style cross-reference, keep it to one short sentence and prefer:
  "For further details on the accounting policies of the Group, please see Note 3 of the Accountants' Report."
  Adjust only the issuer term, note number, and report reference to match the evidence.

DO NOT
- Do not analyse performance, liquidity, cash flow, ratios, or trends.
- Do not reproduce statement tables.
- Do not use generic period wording such as "latest two financial years", "latest three financial years", "shorter period", "all periods presented", or "the respective financial years presented" unless expressly disclosed.
- Do not infer that there are no peculiar accounting policies unless the evidence expressly supports it.
- Do not summarise Note 3 or other accounting-policy content beyond a short referral sentence unless the approved wording for this issuer expressly requires more.
- Do not use bland fallback openings such as "The Group's historical financial information comprises ..." or "Our Group's historical financial information comprises ..." when the approved prospectus-style intro is supported.
- Do not rewrite the first paragraph into accountant-report syntax such as "as at [date]" or "for the financial years ended [date]" when the approved style reference supports the shorter chapter-introduction framing.
- Do not force cash flows, statements of changes in equity, audit-qualification wording, or generic Note 3 referral wording when not supported.
- Do not omit the peculiar-accounting-policies sentence when the selected evidence expressly supports it.
- Do not continue into section 12.1.1.
